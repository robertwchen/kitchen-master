# Failure Modes (Initial List)

1. Partial foot occlusion by player body or racket.
2. Occlusion from net tape, net post, or sideline equipment.
3. Motion blur during rapid step transitions.
4. Defocus blur due to camera autofocus lag.
5. Low-light noise causing boundary ambiguity.
6. Harsh shadows crossing the kitchen line.
7. Glare or reflective court surfaces washing out line paint.
8. Worn or faded kitchen line markings.
9. Camera tilt or roll creating geometric misalignment.
10. Non-ideal camera height causing severe perspective distortion.
11. Frame cropping that excludes critical foot-line contact region.
12. Compression artifacts from low-bitrate recording.
13. Similar shoe and court colors reducing edge contrast.
14. Multiple players entering frame and confusing foot association.
