# Canonical Scenarios

## 1) Clear Legal

A foot is visibly and completely behind the kitchen line with clear separation between shoe boundary and line paint.

## 2) On Line

A foot visibly touches the line paint at any point, with enough image evidence to distinguish contact from near-contact.

## 3) Over Line

A foot clearly extends beyond the line onto the NVZ side, with identifiable boundary crossing.

## 4) Ambiguous

Image conditions or geometry prevent reliable determination among legal/touching/over states; classify as `uncertain`.
